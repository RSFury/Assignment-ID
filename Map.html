Maps



There are a total of 12 Maps in Phasmophobia. They are normally catergories in 3 ways.

Small, Medium and Large Maps. Ranging from 10 to over 50 rooms in a map.

Breakers

Each map have a breaker which can be located on the map.

This to switch on the lights for better navigation around the map.

You can switch the breaker on from red to green to power the location.





Small Maps







6 Tangle Wood Drive

Tangle Wood is one of the favourite maps for beginners. With its small map area and vast number of hiding spots

It is easily ranked one of the easiest maps to be played in.

It consist of 2 floor where the breaker is either found in the basement or the garadge.









42 Edgefield Road

Edgefield house is a small house with complex map design. It is normally played

when beginners are starting to get the hang of the game.

It has 3 floors where the breaker is normally found in the basement or the third floor.









10 Ridgeview Court

Ridgeview house is a personal favourite house of mine where despite being a small house,

it has a vast amount of space for players to run and hide, being a top map to teach beginners.

It has 3 floors with the breaker located in the garadge and basement.









13 Willow Street

Willow Steet house is by far the easiest map in the entire game.

With only 10 rooms and multiple hiding spots depsite the difficulty,

It is easy for beginners to start playing the game even without any guidance.

The house have 2 floors with the breaker located in the basement or the garadge.









Camp Woodwind

Camp Woodwind, the latest small map created on date is by far the hardest

small map created in the game. With it being a smaller version of maple campsite

and the low number of hiding spots available, new players are easily trapped when

the ghost starts to hunt.

The breaker is located near the campfire.



Medium Maps







Maple Lodge Campsite

Maple Campsite is one of the harder medium maps to play, with the map's enourmous area

and low hiding spots available, it is one of the last map you want to play when you are

getting used to the game. Unforviging mistakes and scary consequences.

The map have 2 floors 1 being the camp site 2nd being the house at the campsite.

The breaker is located near the campfire.









Grafton Farmhouse

Grafton Farmhouse is one of the two farmhouses in game. It has enough room to make the game

challening as well as enough hiding spots for newer players to stay alive in.

The breaker is located at the far end of the house or the second floor utilities room.









Bleasdale Farmhouse

The Bleasdale Farmhouse is one two farmhouses in Phasmophobia. It has 3 floors leading to the

attic. It is one of few maps played by very experience players due to low number of hiding spots

While being a place hard to navigate.

The beaker can be located at the first floor and the attic.









Sunny Meadows Restricted

Sunny Meadows Resticted is the only map in game where the play area is constantly customized.

It is a smaller version of Sunny Meadows where doors are blocked to make the map smaller for

Players to wants to play the May but do not want to explore too much to find the ghost room.

The breaker room can be located at multiple locations normally at the utilities room.



Big Maps







Sunny Meadows

Sunny Meadows is the latest map added in the game reworked version of the all time favourite Asylum map.

It is one of the biggest map in game with over 60 rooms and two different floors.

It is loved by many Phasmophobia professionals who wants to challenge themselves with the large map

which is easily 5 times bigger than medium maps.

The breaker room for this map is randomised and is rarely found easily.









Brownstone High School

Brownstone Highschool, the least scary Large map is an abandoned high school with two different floors.

The breaker room , as far as I know of, is only located at the bottom of the stairs leading to second floor.

It is a good map to start of you are playing a large map for the first time.









Prison

Prison is by far the scariest map in Phasmophobia. With the large area of the map with scary looking prison bars,

finding the ghost room is a task even professionals find hard to do. With the added thought of it being a

place for prisoners adds another layer of reason why you should not enter this place.

The breaker room is randomised and is rarely turn on due to the large map.
