Objects

What are Objects?

Objects are things in the map where the ghost can interact with, either touching, throwing and even closing doors.
After any ghost interactions, you are able to locate it with the help of a EMF reader.

Cursed Objects

Cursed Objects are items located at a fixed location in everymap. Their purpose is to make the game
fun and interesting depends on how you use it. There are a total of 6 different Cursed object in the
game. Each map spawns 1 Cursed Object (Unless playing on Sunny Meadows).



Cursed Mirror

The Cursed Mirror is by far the most useful Cursed Object to find the Ghost room.
The Mirror allows the user to see the point of view of the ghost to find the ghost
by matching the surrounding appearance in exchange for the player's sanity.
When the player's sanity is 0, the mirror will crack and the ghost will start Hunting.




Music Box

Music Box is one of the harder cursed object to use. It is used by playing it and
listening to the ghost's singing to find the ghost room. When in used, the ghost will
appear and sing while standing still. When placed on the ground NEAR the ghost
the ghost will walk close till he touches the music box and start a hunt.
If placed far from the ghost, the hunt will start instantly.




Ouija Board

The Ouija Board is a item where allows the player to talk and ask the ghost question.
The question can be anything and the game makes it so that the ghost will responds to
them. Players need to use the in game voice chat to talk to the ghost.
While talking to the ghost, you can take a picture of the ghost interacting with the board.
It exchanges sanity for answers and breaks when a player is too far from the board without saying goodbye
or uses with 0 sanity. This starts a hunt immediately.



Summoning Circle

The Summoning Circle is the most thrilling cursed object to use. It is used to summon the ghost to
get a photo of the ghost. After summoning by lighting the five candles around the circle, the ghost appears
for a few seconds before it starts chasing players.



Tarot Cards

Tarot cards is a deck of cards containing 10 different type of cards with each cards doing different things.
This makes the game fun when you and your friends take turns to draw a card, seeing who's the unlucky one.
The Tower : Causes an interaction

The Wheel of Fortune: The player gains 25% sanity if the card burns green, or loses 25% sanity if the card burns red.

The Fool : Will appear as another card at first, before turning into The Fool as it burns. No effect is applied when drawing this card.

The Devil : Triggers a Ghost Event towards the nearest player to the ghost.

Death : Trigger a Ghost Hunt

The Hermit : Teleports the ghost back to its ghost room and traps it there for 1 minute. This does not affect hunts.

The Sun : Fully restores sanity to 100%.

The Moon : Instantly drops sanity to 0%.

The High Priestess :Revives a randomly chosen dead player at their corpse.
If no one is currently dead, it will revive the next player who dies. Multiple cards do not stack.
If a player dies during a hunt and is revived, they cannot be killed again in the same hunt.

The Hanged Man : Instantly kills the player. This card will always turn into The Fool if the 'Friendly Ghost' custom difficulty option is turned on.






VooDoo Doll

The VooDoo Doll is by far the most underwhelming Cursed Object. It simply stabs the doll with needle and when it its the heart.
A ghost hunt starts. Other colour needles trigger ghost interactions and that about it.
