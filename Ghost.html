Ghosts

There are many different type of Ghost in the game. Each have their own conditions and

UNIQUE hidden ability and will be listed down below.

Legend:

Evidence = The evidence to identify the Ghost

Hunt Threshold = Sanity level for Hunt to occur.(Individual)

Give Away = Things ONLY that Ghost do.

Excluder = Things ONLY that Ghost do not do.

Hidden Ability = Things the Ghost can do while others could not. (mainly interactions)

Notes = Obvious things that ghost do MORE/LESS compared to other ghost.





Spirit

Evidence : EMF 5, Spirit Box , Ghost Writing

Hunt Threshold : 50%

Hidden Ability : Smudging the Room prevents hunts for 3 Minutes

(rather than the normal 1.5 Minutes)





Wraith

Evidence : EMF 5, Spirit Box, DOTS

Hunt Threshold : 50%

Give Away : No Salt Footprints

Hidden Ability : May Teleport to Player (Giving of EMF Readings)

Notes : Random EMF response at player outside of Ghost Room or Roaming Distance





Phantom

Evidence : Spirit Box, Fingerprints, DOTS

Hunt Threshold: 50%

Give Away : Disappears after taking Picture & Ghost Picture has NO static in it

Hidden Ability : Reduces Sanity when you look at it

Notes : Hunt: The Ghost Model with flash in only every 1-2 seconds,

rather than every 0.3s-1s (meaning slower flashes)





Poltergeist

Evidence : Spirit Box, Fingerprints, Writing

Hunt Threshold : 50%

Give Away : Multithrow & Far-Throw

Hidden Ability : Can throw more than one object occasionally

& Can throw objects further than other ghosts.

Notes : Touches everything constantly somehow.

During Hunts it throws most shit it comes in contact with.

(consider kiting through Writing Book)





Banshee

Evidence : Orbs, Fingerprints, DOTS

Hunt Threshold : 50%(Target's Sanity)

Give Away : Somehow ignores closer players during a Hunt & Scream on Paramic

Hidden Ability : Prefers Singing Ghost Events

Notes : Banshee will pic a random target, and only go for them during a hunt.

It will also only check the target's Sanity for the Hunt threshhold.

If it's target is outside the Investigation Area, the Banshee will act like a normal ghost.





Jinn

Evidence : EMF 5, Freezing, Fingerprints

Hunt Threshold : 50%

Excluder : Turned off the Breaker (directly) &

jumps closer to players in sight

Hidden Ability : May reduce the Sanity of nearby players by 25% IF the Breaker is on

Notes : The Jinn's ability to reduce your Sanity, will give an EMF reading at the breaker.





Mare

Evidence : Orbs, Spirit Box, Writing

Hunt Threshold : Light Off: 60% & Light On: 40%

Give Away : Light turns off immediately after being turned on

Excluder : Turned on a Light

Hidden Ability : May sometimes turn off a light immediately after a player turns it on

Notes : Mares will roam further if the light in their room is turned on.





Revenant

Evidence : Orbs, Freezing, Writing

Hunt Threshold : 50%

Give Away : Hunt & Salt: Slow footsteps

Excluder : Fast Footsteps

Notes : 2 Different Hunt Speeds.

When it sees a player: GIGA FAST

When it sees no player: Really Slow





Shade

Evidence : EMF 5, Freezing, Writing

Hunt Threshold : 35%

Excluder : Hunt starts in the same room as player.

Hidden Ability : Unable to do Throws, Ghost Events or Hunts when another player is in a room with her &

Can Appear as Black Shadow in Summoning Circle

Notes : Incredibly shy and inactive





Demon

Evidence : Freezing, Fingerprints, Writing

Hunt Threshold : 70%(100% Ability)

Hidden Ability : May (regardless of Sanity) walk towards a random player, then initiate a hunt

Crucifixes have 5m Radius

Notes : Somehow all the Crucifixes are gone & smudging them only prevents hunts for 1 Minute.





Yurei

Evidence : Orbs, Freezing, DOTS

Hunt Threshold : 50%

Give Away : The Yurei Door Close: He will touch a door and a second later close it fully (2 sounds) &

Everyone's sanity is oddly low

Hidden Ability : Ability that reduces the Sanity of nearby players (Door Shut) &

More likely to do Mistball Ghost Events

Notes : When you hear a door touch, with a suspiciously delayed door closing sound

outside of Ghost Events and Hunts. Smudging the ghost will trap it in it's room

for the entire Smudge Duration (90s)





Oni

Evidence : EMF 5, Freezing, DOTS

Hunt Threshold : 50%

Excluder : Mistball Ghost Event

Hidden Ability : Flickers less during Hunts & Drains sanity during Ghost Events at twice the speed

Notes : Flickers less during hunts, is visible longer (opposite of Phantom).

Will more often appear in full form during an event.





Yokai

Evidence : Orbs, Spirit Box, DOTS

Hunt Threshold : 50% (80% When someone talks close to them.)

Give Away : Unable to find you during a hunt despite active electric devices

Notes : During hunts, Yokais cannot hear voices or notice electrical devices unless

they are in it's direct vicinity (2-3m).

Talking within 2-3m of them may trigger a hunt.





Hantu

Evidence : Orbs, Freezing, Fingerprints

Hunt Threshold : 50%

Excluder : No Freezing Temperature Evidence

Hidden Ability : Has frozen breath when hunting in her own room & cannot turn on breaker

Notes : Guarantees Freezing Temperatures on Nightmare & Chance to turn off the breaker is doubled





Goryo

Evidence : EMF 5, Fingerprints, DOTS

Hunt Threshold : 50%

Excluder : DOTS seen without a camera & No DOTS Evidence

Notes : Guarantees DOTS evidence on Nightmare

DOTS evidence can only be seen through a camera and outside the Ghost Room

Will rarely roam outside the Ghost Room





Myling

Evidence : EMF 5, Fingerprints, Writing

Hunt Threshold : 50%

Hidden Ability : Higher Chance for Singing Ghost Events & Hunt's Footstep Sound range is

lower than Flashlight flicker range

Notes : Mylings being quieter during hunts refers ONLY to the footstep sound.

It is shorter than the Electrics malfunction range. So sit in a hiding space with a flashlight

on the ground, if you hear the footsteps (on the same level) before the flashlight goes off, it's a Myling





Onryo

Evidence : Orbs, Spirit Box, Freezing

Hunt Threshold : 60%

Excluder : Consumes Crucifixes despite a burning candle on top of them

Notes : Onryo Test: Place a crucifix and then a candle on top of it. When trying to hunt but a Candle is within 4 meters,

the candle will be blown out and the hunt stopped.

If it consumes the cross without blowing off the candle, it's not an Onryo





The Twins

Evidence : EMF 5, Spirit Box, Freezing

Hunt Threshold : 50%

Give Away : Two different interaction at different rooms.

Hidden Ability : Both Ghosts can hunt.

Main Ghost: 10% LESS speed

Decoy: 10% MORE speed

Notes : Twinteraction: refers to event where on the Van activity meter there are two different slope angles in one reading.

Note if two interactions happen close to each other in time, but in multiple different rooms a single ghost could not reach so quickly.





Raiju

Evidence : EMF 5, Orbs, DOTS

Hunt Threshold : 50% (65% when nearby electrical equipment)

Give Away : During Hunts, Ghost Speed increases and decreases during the hunt (when nearby active electronic devices)

Hidden Ability : Moves faster around Electrical Equipment

Notes : When electrical devices (player items) are in it's ghost room, the hunt threshold is increased.

During hunts, being near active electric devices (brought by players) will speed the ghost up.

Entering and leaving the vicinity of these devices will sound as if the ghost speed changes dynamically.





Obake

Evidence : EMF 5, Orbs, Fingerprints

Hunt Threshold : 50%

Give Away : Hand prints with 6 fingers or two thumbs on a light switch

Excluder : No Fingerprints Evidence

Hidden Ability : Only has a 75% chance to leave Fingerprints for an interaction

Notes : Guarantees Fingerprints on Nightmare





The Mimic

Evidence : Orbs, Spirit Box, Freezing, Fingerprints

Hunt Threshold : Depends on mimiced Ghost

Give Away : 3 Evidences on Nightmare, 4 on Professional

Excluder : No Ghost Orbs Evidence

Hidden Ability : Can copy the behaviour of every other ghost

Notes : Always produces Ghost Orb Evidence, no matter what the journal says





Moroi

Evidence : Spirit Box, Freezing, Writing

Hunt Threshold : 50%

Give Away : Curse: Spirit Box or Paramic response will drop your sanity despite using a candle

Excluder : No Spirit Box Evidence

Hidden Ability : Hunt speed depends on player sanity when the hunt starts

Notes : Guarantees Spirit Box Evidence on Nightmare.

Being cursed (Spirit Box, Paramic response) will double your sanity drain, while also ignoring the candle protection





Deogen

Evidence : Spirit Box, Writing, DOTS

Hunt Threshold : 40%

Give Away : GIGA FAST when you are hidden, omega slow when next to him

Hidden Ability : Using the Spirit Box within 1m of him, will make him respond with a heavy breathing sound

(If Spirit Box is an Evidence Type)

Notes : You CANNOT HIDE from this ghost, you will need to loop or kite it in the open.





Thaye

Evidence : Orbs, Writing, DOTS

Hunt Threshold : 75% - 15%(-6% per aging step)

Excluder : Low Activity / Slow Hunt Speed early on

Hidden Ability : Constantly Ages

Starts hyperactive and fast, becomes slower and less active over time

Notes : Every 2 Minutes the ghost attempts to age when you are close, if not the timer will be increased by 30 seconds.

Be in the ghost room to make him age.
